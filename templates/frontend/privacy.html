We collect two kinds of information from you: personally-identifiable information such as your name, email address, telephone number and payment information, that you choose to provide to us and non personally-identifiable information, such as your IP address.

An IP Address is a number used by computers on a network to identify your computer so that data can be transmitted to you. We collect IP Address information in order to administer our web site and to gather broad demographic information.

Information You Provide. We receive and store information such as registration and payment information and resumes, licenses or other profile information that you provide on the Website. The only personally-identifiable information that we collect is what you voluntarily submit to us. It is your choice whether to provide us with such information. If you choose not to provide us with certain information, you may not be able to subscribe to our services or become a member, use certain features of the Website and our services, including the bartending and wait staff profile/listing services, locating a local bartender or wait staff, or accessing our databases

For example, if you register for our services or set-up a profile, e-mail questions or requests for information about our services, or make a payment for our services, we may request your name, mailing address, credit card information, telephone number and/or e-mail address in order to respond to your inquiries or process your registration or set-up your database profile. Information Collected Automatically – Cookies. We automatically receive and store certain types of information whenever you use the Website in the form of “cookies”.1 A “cookie” is a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from a web server and stored on your computer’s hard drive. Cookies enable the Website to recognize you and to identify repeat visitors and help us improve your access to our Website. You may configure your web browser to not accept cookies through your browser’s settings, but doing so may require you to re-enter your information more often when using our Website and certain Website features may not function properly.
